01 — GROWTH TRAJECTORY

AI postings outpaced every other category

After a tepid 2022, AI-related job postings exploded through 2024 driven by the enterprise adoption wave following generative AI breakthroughs. The surge was not limited to Big Tech — mid-market companies and financial services saw the sharpest percentage increases.

02 — GEOGRAPHY

The new AI talent corridors

San Francisco remains dominant, but the map is shifting. New York surged on the back of finance and media adoption. Austin and Seattle are emerging as genuine alternatives as companies diversify away from Bay Area cost structures.

03 — INDUSTRY BREAKDOWN

Finance overtook healthcare as the #2 hirer

Technology still dominates in absolute volume, but the fastest-growing sectors are finance, legal services, and manufacturing — industries with historically low AI adoption that are now racing to close the gap.
